Tokenomics

HURRICANE has a limited issue of 25 billion coins!

Our goal was to create a project that doesn't just multiply capital in a second, but that the long holding of the token brings a constant profit, so the HURRICANE smart contract has a few unique features:

1. First, it is a feature to distribute a portion of the funds from each purchase or sale of HURRICANE on the exchange to other wallets.

2. Second, it is a function of burning a portion of the tokens in the liquidity pool each time SHUMI is bought or sold on the exchange, reducing the total volume of tokens issued and increasing their value in the wallets of the holders.
