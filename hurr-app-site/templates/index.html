Tokenomics

HURRICANE has a limited issue of 25 billion coins!

Our goal was to create a project that doesn't just multiply capital in a second, but that the long holding of the token brings a constant profit, so the HURRICANE smart contract has a few unique features:

· First, it is a feature to distribute a portion of the funds from each purchase or sale of HURRICANE on the exchange to other wallets.

· Second, it is a function of burning a portion of the tokens in the liquidity pool each time HURRICANE is bought or sold on the exchange, reducing the total volume of tokens issued and increasing their value in the wallets of the holders.

F&Q

We have high growth prospects for those who decide to invest in the first days after going public. Also, we are planning to develop our coin due to the fact that we will not be tied to any currency, meaning the rate will mainly depend on the supply/demand balance and the global cryptocurrency market situation. We want that in the future the coin can be used as a currency to pay for goods and services. The development vectors are different and promising!
We are using opportunity to buy HURRICANE coin through smart contracts which are known for high level of transaction security. The smart contracts will be based on the Ethereum blockchain platform. Also, the security of investments can be ensured by sending funds to stack. Stacking is a way of passive earning in which users store coins on the Proof of Stake (PoS) algorithm and ensure that the blockchain works.
There is no threshold of entry. But in the future, we plan to release exclusives in different kinds (like NFT in our game). These exclusives will be available only to those who prove their confidence in us by having a certain minimum amount on their wallet in the form of our coin.
We are going to use automated analysis to get supply and demand data on the cryptocurrency market soon. Right now, according to our calculations, the potential yield can reach more than 20% in the medium term. We don't want to promise a quick return of a few hundred percent, as we first care about the coin being liquid and hence not exposed to high volatility and high risk to our investors' investments.
The game is based on a system of play and earn. The game has an in-game store, where you can buy tokenized skins and unique items. You can also get these items after completing levels. All these items are divided by season, which multiplies their value soon. And most importantly, the main currency in our game is our coin.
All our products and services that will be released regularly can only be purchased for the HURRICANE coin!
